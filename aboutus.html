About us.

Let There Be Cats (LTBC) is all about feeding these cats and helping people provide for their companions. That's our purpose and objective.

LTBC was started to serve as a stepping stone for a much bigger project.We are here to create a positive experience for pet owners and their beloved pets. We are raising funds for our virtual pet food bank and to invest in opportunities that grow cash flow to begin the process of becoming self sufficient cat sanctuary. We don't just want to spend, collect, provide, solicit then repeat, We want make sure that we multiply that resource so that we remain reliable for you all!
