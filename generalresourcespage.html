Frequently Asked Questions

How does the program work?

We are a new virtual pet food bank that provides assistance for pet owners in need. You can apply for assistance using this form. You will need an amazon wishlist to submit the form. Create an amazon wishlist for pet related items and share your amazon wishlist link in the form, then submit. After review, we purchase items on your amazon wishlist.

What items can be added to the wishlist?

Toys, food, litter, bedding, flea and worm treatments can be added to your wishlist.

How long do you provide assistance?

3 months.
