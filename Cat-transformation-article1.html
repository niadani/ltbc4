Can Cats Drink Alkaline Water?.

by Nia Daniels | May 3, 2021 | Story
Greetings and the overall answer is no, my dear.

**Why is the answer no?**

The answer is no because cat food already contains magnesium, so you would be providing them with extra magnesium ( an abundant mineral in the body ). This would cause an imbalance causing issues with digestion, brain function and other issues that you won’t encounter after reading the answer to your question! This isn’t something you would give them daily or 99% of the time.

**Still curious?**

Curious is the cat. If there is a magnesium deficiency, which is considered rare, you could provide a multivitamin made for cats, and/or follow your vets recommendation, since you would most likely know for sure if they have a magnesium deficiency from your vet! Side note: There are anecdotes of cats urinating more than usual when given alkaline water.

**What kind of water should I give my cat?**

**(What I recommend)**

Improving the quality of your tap water.

**(2nd recommendation)**

Distilled water or Spring water

Cat water.

Tap water. Link to check your city’s water and see what's in it. Just like low quality water can give humans health issues, it can affect cats as well.
